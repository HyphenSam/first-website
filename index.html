Ohayō~

Azu-nyan is hands down best girl in K-On!.

She is a reliable, smart, and hard working girl, but isn't too strict and enjoys chilling out with the other cast every now and then.
She also seems to have a very distinct pair of eyes compared to the other characters, remonisant to a cat's. This is especially fitting to her nickname that Yui has understandably chosen.

She's the only character with a fantastic character progression throughout the series, and it concluded fantastically. It also has a very good message behind it, which is rare in generic slice-of-life anime.
On top of being absolutely adorable—especially with cat-ears—she easily earns the number one spot for me.

Top defining features of Azu-nyan~

  1. Smart, reliable, and hard-working
  2. Cat-like eyes
  3. Small stature
  4. Cute twin-tails
  5. Flat chest
